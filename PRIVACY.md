# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-16

This privacy policy covers the ADI ATP Plugin for Claude Code and the ATP platform at [atp.bigsmallai.com](https://atp.bigsmallai.com), operated by BigSmall AI ("we", "us").

## What we collect

When you install the plugin and authenticate via OAuth 2.1:

- **Account identity** — your email address and OAuth-issued user ID.
- **Query content** — the prompts and parameters you send when invoking a plugin skill (e.g., feature descriptions, scenarios, design questions).
- **Generated outputs** — the reports, evaluations, and analyses produced by ATP in response to your queries, stored under your account as thread history.
- **Usage metadata** — timestamps, skill names, token counts, and billing events.

We do not collect browser cookies or behavioral analytics through the plugin itself.

## How we use it

- To run the skill you invoked and deliver the report back to you.
- To maintain your thread history so you can retrieve past runs.
- To bill usage against your account (for paid tiers, if applicable).
- To diagnose errors and improve service quality.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your queries or reports to train models.

## Third parties

Running a skill involves sending query content to the following processors:

- **OpenAI** and **Anthropic** — LLM providers. Query content and retrieved context are sent to their APIs to generate analysis. Subject to their respective privacy policies and data-use terms.
- **DigitalOcean** — hosts the ATP backend and PostgreSQL database where your threads and reports are stored.
- **Stripe** (paid tiers only) — processes payments. We never see your card details.

No other third parties receive your data.

## Retention

- **Thread history and reports** — retained until you delete them or close your account.
- **Billing records** — retained as required by applicable tax and accounting law.
- **Logs** — operational logs (not containing query content) are retained for up to 30 days.

## Your rights

- **Access** — view all your threads via the ATP dashboard.
- **Delete** — delete individual threads, or close your account to remove all associated data.
- **Export** — request a copy of your data by emailing us.

## Security

- OAuth 2.1 authentication — no API keys stored in the plugin.
- TLS for all traffic between the plugin, ATP backend, and third parties.
- Database access restricted to ATP service processes.

## Changes

We will update this page when the policy changes. Material changes will be announced via email to registered users.
